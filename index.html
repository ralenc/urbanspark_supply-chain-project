Why the simulated baseline ≠ observed 33
Two simplifications cause the model to be conservative
DEMAND UNDERESTIMATION
Bootstrap resampling from only 12 historical months understates tail demand events. More data → better tail estimates.
LEAD-TIME VARIABILITY IGNORED
The simulation assumes orders always arrive exactly on schedule. Real supplier delays add additional stockout risk not captured here.
Calibration approach: Since both runs (as-is and to-be) share the same simplifications, the relative reduction is more reliable than absolute counts. Applying −74% to the observed baseline of 33 gives the projected KPI15 figure of ~9 events/year.
